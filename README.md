**Your Mission:**
Race against the clock to deliver scattered parcels in a compact, handcrafted city. You have only **70 seconds** to prove you are the fastest courier in town!

**How to Play:**
1. **Pick Up:** Find the glowing **Yellow Parcel** to start your mission.
2. **Drop Off:** Head straight to the **Green Zone** to complete the delivery.
3. **The Goal:** Successfully deliver **5 packages** before the timer hits zero to win!
